Our Guiding Principles

How I apply Deutsche Telekom's guiding principles in my daily work

Delight Our Customers

Putting customer needs at the center of everything I do

Situation:

Developers across Deutsche Telekom needed faster, more efficient ways to code, but AI adoption was low and inconsistent.

Task:

Increase enterprise-wide adoption of AI developer tools to improve developer experience and productivity.

Action:

Get Things Done

Delivering results with focus, accountability, and execution excellence

Situation:

COVID-19 testing registration in Košice region required hours of manual processing, creating bottlenecks and delays for citizens.

Task:

Rapidly develop and deploy a digital solution to streamline COVID-19 testing registration for public health emergency.

Action:

Led development team in rapid delivery of "eRegistracia" application, managing technical implementation and coordinating with health authorities.

Result:

Reduced registration time from hours to minutes. Team received Badge of City Košice from the Mayor for community impact.

Learning:

In crisis situations, rapid execution with clear focus on user needs creates transformative impact. Speed + quality = exceptional outcomes.
