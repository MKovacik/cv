Delight Our Customers

Putting customer needs at the center of everything I do

Situation:

Our internal developer customers needed simple, innovative tools to improve their coding experience, but existing AI tools had low adoption and developers weren't achieving their productivity goals.

Task:

Understand different developer views and needs, then design and deliver a human-centered AI solution that would help them achieve their goals while challenging the status quo of traditional development approaches.

Action:

I incorporated developers' point of view through listening tours and feedback sessions, challenged internal processes by advocating for GitHub Copilot adoption despite initial resistance, and created simple, innovative experiences through company-wide AI awareness campaigns reaching 4,000+ employees. I passionately championed understanding customer needs and served developers fairly and equally across all teams.

Result:

Helped developers achieve their productivity goals - grew active user base by 21% to over 3,000 developers generating 5.3+ million lines of code. Created trusted companion relationship with our internal customers, demonstrating measurable ROI to VTI Management Board.

Learning:

Being a trusted companion means putting customer needs at the heart of everything - understanding their views, challenging barriers that hinder their needs, and co-creating solutions that help them achieve their goals through simple, innovative and human-centered experiences.

Task:

Deliver a fast, high-quality solution by thinking end-to-end from citizen registration to health authority data management, staying ahead of the crisis through entrepreneurial, solution-oriented action.

Action:

I tackled the challenge proactively and promptly, leading the team to go straight for the solution without waiting for perfect requirements. We went for it - started small with MVP and continuously improved based on real user feedback. I thought end-to-end about the entire registration flow, coordinating with health authorities while maintaining entrepreneurial spirit to overcome bureaucratic obstacles.

Result:

Delivered fast and high-quality solution - reduced registration time from hours to minutes. Our execution-focused, solution-oriented approach helped us stay ahead, earning the team Badge of City Košice from the Mayor for community impact.

Learning:

Being entrepreneurial and solution-oriented means tackling challenges proactively, going for it even without perfect conditions, and thinking end-to-end to deliver fast, high-quality solutions. This mindset helps us stay ahead of competition and make things happen when it matters most.

Action:

I committed to upholding our values by creating clear, objective criteria that treated everyone fairly. Learning:

Our actions define us - taking a transparent stance on important issues like fairness and diversity creates lasting positive change.
